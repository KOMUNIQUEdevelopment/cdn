KOMUNIQUE — About us

We Encourage Clients to Trust Their Digital Vision and Build Something Unique.

Philipp Roth: The Visionary Behind KOMUNIQUE.

KOMUNIQUE, is a full-service web development agency nestled in the heart of Basel. We are driven by a passion for innovation, a commitment to excellence, and a deep understanding of the digital landscape. At KOMUNIQUE, we don't just build websites; we craft digital experiences that resonate, captivate, and inspire.

Dive in to discover our story, our ethos, and the unique blend of expertise that sets us apart in the bustling world of web development.

The DNA of KOMUNIQUE

At the heart of KOMUNIQUE lies a relentless pursuit of innovation, collaboration, and excellence. Our name, a blend of "KOM" from "Kommunikation" (German for "Communication") and "UNIQUE", encapsulates our ethos perfectly: delivering standout, unique communication solutions. We believe in pushing boundaries, in redefining the norms, and in creating digital experiences that resonate. This blend of tradition and modernity, depth and dynamism, is what sets KOMUNIQUE apart in the digital landscape.

Our Journey

KOMUNIQUE wasn’t born overnight. It’s the evolution of a journey that began with HeadStarterz, a full-service marketing agency with a focus on the legal market. Founded in 2016 by Philipp Roth and co-owned with Tobias Steinemann since 2018, HeadStarterz carved a niche for itself in the realm of marketing. As the digital landscape evolved, so did our vision. Recognizing the need for specialized, top-tier web development, KOMUNIQUE was born. We are taking forward the legacy of web development from HeadStarterz and act as a dedicated development partner, collaborating closely on key projects. Philipp now leads KOMUNIQUE as a full-service web development business under this new framework.

Our Expertise

‍KOMUNIQUE is more than just a name; it's a testament to our expertise. Specializing in Webflow, Craft CMS, and FlutterFlow, we prefer visual programming and development to expedite the development process. But when needed, we're also adept at writing code line by line. Our founder, Philipp Roth, is an experienced expert in these platforms, bringing several years of experience in website and app development to the table.

Collaborative Spirit

‍We're not a one-man show. KOMUNIQUE thrives on collaboration. Our team and extensive network of freelancers and partners, each a maestro in their respective domain, from design to front-end and back-end development, to content creation, ensures that we deliver nothing but the best.

Sustainability

‍In an era of digital transformation, we recognize the importance of sustainability. Our commitment extends beyond just creating sustainable web designs. We're dedicated to making a positive impact, ensuring our operations and projects are environmentally conscious.

KOMUNIQUE is more than just a brand. It's a statement. With our expertise, we're not just setting the bar; we're redefining it. We're here to innovate, captivate, and elevate the world of web design. If you're looking for groundbreaking web design, KOMUNIQUE is where your search ends.

Philipp Roth, Founder and CEO

Frequently Asked.

What is your process for managing website projects?

Our process is systematic and client-focused. We start with a discovery phase, understanding your business objectives and target audience. Then we move to design, creating mockups and wireframes that represent the look and feel of the final site. Upon design approval, we transition to development, coding the site to be responsive and optimized. After thorough testing, we proceed to launch, with ongoing maintenance and support as required.

How much experience does your agency and its network of professionals have in web development?

Our agency itself boasts a long-standing experience in web development. Our approach is unique in that we collaborate with a vast network of experts, consisting of seasoned freelancers and partners, each bringing their distinct expertise. This collective has a wealth of experience, having successfully executed countless web projects across diverse industries. From experienced designers crafting compelling visuals to skilled developers ensuring robust functionality and language experts refining content, our network ensures every facet of web development is expertly addressed.

What coding languages and frameworks does your agency specialize in?

We take great pride in maintaining our expertise with cutting-edge web technologies. Our proficiency encompasses core web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as renowned frameworks including React, Dart, and Flutter. For backend solutions, we prefer CMS platforms such as Webflow, Craft CMS, and Netlify CMS. Moreover, we seamlessly integrate cloud-based processes where appropriate to optimize and enhance web functionality.

What steps do you take to ensure a website meets professional standards?

We adhere to best practices in every phase. This includes creating responsive designs for all devices, optimizing for speed and SEO, implementing secure protocols, and conducting rigorous testing across different browsers and devices. Post-launch, we monitor the site's performance and offer ongoing support to address any issues or updates.

How do you incorporate client feedback during the website development process?

Client collaboration is pivotal. After the initial design mockups are prepared, we solicit feedback to ensure alignment with your vision. Regular check-ins and reviews are scheduled throughout the development phase. We offer multiple revision rounds, ensuring the final product resonates with your brand and meets your business objectives.

Our Values that we work by

Collaboration

We believe that working with others is essential to creating great work. By collaborating with our clients and the dev community, we can share ideas, perspectives, and expertise to create something truly outstanding.

Beautiful

Beauty is an important component of any project. Whether it's a website, an app, or a physical product, it should be visually appealing and easy to use.

Long-lasting

We believe that products should be built to last. By focusing on durability and quality, we can create products that stand the test of time.

Sustainable

It's important to design visually appealing, functional, environmentally friendly, and sustainable websites and apps. This can be achieved by reducing energy consumption, using eco-friendly hosting services, and creating a better future for generations to come.

Detailed

We believe that attention to detail is critical in creating great work. By focusing on the little things, we can create products that are polished, refined, and truly exceptional.

Balanced

We think that balance is key in life and in work. By finding a healthy balance between work and play, creativity and structure, and ambition and humility, We can achieve the goals and create meaningful work.
